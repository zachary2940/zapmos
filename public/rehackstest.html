About

Project

  • Teams will come up with projects related to the theme SmartCampus in NTU.
  • Teams are encouraged to not reuse any projects and build something new!
  • Projects will be judged based on Creativeness & Impressiveness, Usefulness, Technical Difficulties, Business Potential, and Theme Relevance.
  • Hardware hacks are welcome! Loaning of hardware parts are available upon request.

Eligibility

  • Any REP student from any specialisation is welcomed to participate.
  • Admission is free for everyone and meals will be provided. (Only finale Lunch is provided)
  • Participants will form teams of at most 3 members.
